Why us?

1. Live and Interactive
We specialize in offering one-on-one live classes tailored specifically for your child. Unlike online courses, live classes are much more effective as real time interaction helps develop interpersonal skills. We encourage creative solutions and logical thinking methodologies in our classes.

2. Personalized Learning
Your kid will have a personal instructor who will stay with them throughout the duration of the course, giving a real learning experience. Because of this, the instructor would also learn about your kid’s needs and concentrate on areas they struggle with.

3. Learn Anything
From coding to music, we cover a wide range of subjects, from programming to music, with a plethora of courses under each topic! CMS Online Academy is a one-stop-shop for all your kid's learning needs.
